Project Purple – Neon Purple Mini-Bundle
----------------------------------------

This bundle contains 5 original 4K wallpapers designed in the
futuristic, neon-purple Project Purple aesthetic. Each image is 
lossless PNG (3840×2160, 16:9).

Wallpapers included:
01_EventideSingularity.png   – Alien horizon glow with a neon sun
02_NeonSprawl.png            – Futuristic city skyline in neon
03_LuminousFlux.png          – Cosmic nebula stream of glowing energy
04_CryovoidHalo.png          – Radiant ice planet with luminous aura
05_EclipseCascade.png        – Galactic sunset ridge with cascading light
